There is cartography in the fracture. You can date it, name it. You can put it on the record or trace the hairline back to its origin event. What you cannot do is decide when it stops being wound and starts being just the shape of you. Good luck trying to stop it.

They do not shatter clean. The soul fractures the way old porcelain does... still holding its form, still useful at the table, just threaded now with lines that show exactly where the cold got in between and forced it apart.
