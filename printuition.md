If it doesn't make money it doesn't make sense;
words spoken by the prophet as he laments.
Realizing the losses and what the profit represents
and the face value of intuition when it prints.

Everyone is talking about how they want progress
but who dares to undergo the alchemical process?
What a person must go through to become after a wrong guess
seeing everything now cost more and knowing which things are costless.

For what do they truly value if not the price?
How thinly can this American pie be sliced?
Call the piper, the president, and the press twice;
roll the dice like three wise men or blind mice.

There is no other foe it is ignorance that's the enemy;
our own foolish pride and who we aren't but pretend to be.
