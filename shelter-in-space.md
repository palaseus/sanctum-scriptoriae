I've experienced so many things alone
and so much of it, I feel, isn't even my own.
that I've begun to think others share in my path
the isolation has me unsure of whether they do or they don't.

The truth can be a hard thing to swallow
when you're actively choking on your pride;
after you've gazed the thousand yards of stare
and the nothing behind their eyes.

Look and see how the hope had died
so much longer before it's arrival
There's a toll you'll pay and cry
in the time when the game is survival.
