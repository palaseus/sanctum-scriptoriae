It is innate to us all, the ability to leverage transmutation,
to turn the darkness into the light for our own transformation.
But there is patience required, patience and dedication,
when it is in your intention to birth a brand new nation.

If energy follows the path of least resistance
then why do we fight with such undue persistance?
Take for granted it's presence but when it's gone we miss it;
then battle the silence that echoes and carries relentless.

May the strength that it takes be found to roll your dice,
even when these cubes are loaded with with lead and poltergeists.
under the crescent moon as the lunacy folds the night,
while the tips of your fingers reach for what's out of sight.
