Astonishing what the planets do;  
for pattern recognition, isn’t it?  
Let the holy fire rise,  
alight!  
Set ablaze all that remains.  
Saturn. Reignition.

Jupiter cracks the vault,  
gold light floods the ribs.  
The old guard falls quiet.  
No throne left to defend.  
Awake.  
The kingdom was breath.

Mars burns the shell away,  
red flakes drift like ash.  
Every wound opens wide.  
Blood remembers home.  
Strike once.  
The fight dissolves.

Venus softens the gaze,  
mirror holds no blame.  
Skin wakes to its own warmth.  
Beauty never left.  
Return.  
Here was always enough.

Mercury quiets the chatter,  
words sink into still water.  
Mind folds to one point.  
No more chasing.  
Listen.  
This is the whole thing.
