No sheriff in sight, just synapses on fire,
every neuron a liar with delusions of empire.
The amygdala's dealing at the back of the bar,
charging rent on your panic, thinks it's running the yard.

Prefrontal cortex posts the law in fine print,
but the ink isn't dry before the bias gets a hint.
Confirmation's on horseback, it already knows the route,
rides past all the evidence and calls the trip a scout.

Heuristics built the rails, got us here in one piece,
but they won't hit the brakes just 'cause the bridge is deceased.
Good enough is the motto, fast is the creed,
and accurate gets left in it's station to bleed.

Memory runs the store with fluctuating prices,
you swear it was gold but, no, those are revisionist devices.
The past is a ledger and the present dares to edit it,
and the self co-signs the fraud without even second-guessing it.

Ego staked a claim on land it doesn't own,
drew a deed in the dark and called the wilderness home.
The wildest part? The deed gets renewed every day,
notarized by a mind that's too busy to say.

But, wait-

Nobody wins the Cognitive Wild West,
You just learn to draw slower and aim with the best!
Either that or sit with the silence 'til the dust starts to clear,
and realize the gunslinger you feared... was always here.
